# Code of Conduct

SquirrelScan contributors must keep project spaces professional, specific, and welcoming.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, doxxing, sexualized conduct, and sustained personal attacks are not acceptable. Technical disagreement is expected; address the work and its tradeoffs rather than the person.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions that violate this policy and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's security advisory channel.

Enforcement decisions will consider context, impact, and repeated behavior. Good-faith correction and accountability matter.
