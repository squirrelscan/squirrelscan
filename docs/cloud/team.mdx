Team is the multi-person plan: invite teammates into an org, assign roles, and share one pool of cloud credits instead of managing credits per person.

## Pricing

| | |
|---|---|
| Price | $29/seat/month, or $290/seat/year (12 months for the price of 10) |
| Minimum seats | 2 |
| Credits | 3,000 per seat, pooled across the org |
| Billing | Monthly or annual; credits arrive monthly either way |

Everything on [Pro](/cloud/credits) carries over: scheduled audits, custom request headers, and render concurrency, raised again to 10 concurrent renders (Pro runs 5). Team's [max pages per cloud audit](/cloud/credits#max-pages-per-cloud-audit) ceiling is also the highest of the three plans, and rises further as report processing scales up.

Sign in and go to **Settings → Team** in the [dashboard](/dashboard) to upgrade, pick a seat count, and check out.

## Pooled credits

A Team org gets one shared credit pool each billing period: `seats x 3,000`. Every member draws from the same pool, monthly credits included with your plan reset each billing period, same as Free and Pro. See [Credits & Pricing](/cloud/credits) for what each cloud feature costs and how spend controls work.

## Seat billing rules

Seats are billed on **accepted** membership, not on invites sent:

- Sending an invite costs nothing. The seat count (and the pooled credit grant) only changes once the invite is **accepted**.
- Removing a member drops the seat count by one, floored at the 2-seat minimum - the org is never billed below the minimum even with fewer active members.
- Seat changes are **prorated**: adding or removing a seat mid-cycle adjusts that period's invoice instead of waiting for renewal.

## Roles

Every member has a role that controls what they can do in an org:

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| Owner | Full access, including billing and org deletion. One per org. |
| Admin | Manage members, websites, and settings. Cannot delete the org. |
| Editor | Create and manage websites and audits. |
| Viewer | Read-only access to reports and audits. |
| Billing | Manage subscription and credits only, no website access. |

Admins and owners invite members and set roles from **Settings → Team**.
